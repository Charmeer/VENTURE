## Key Features Implemented:

### Four Key Risk Categories:

1. **Feedstock Supply Consistency**
   - Risk: Dependence on consistent plastic waste availability from local sources
   - Impact: Operational interruptions and revenue loss
   - Mitigation: Multiple supplier agreements and local collection partnerships

2. **Price Volatility**
   - Risk: Market fluctuations in feedstock costs and product pricing
   - Impact: Reduced profitability and competitiveness issues
   - Mitigation: Long-term supply contracts and diversified customer base

3. **Regulatory Changes**
   - Risk: Evolving environmental regulations and compliance requirements
   - Impact: Increased compliance costs and operational modifications
   - Mitigation: Regular compliance monitoring and industry association membership

4. **Technology Performance**
   - Risk: Equipment reliability issues and technical malfunctions
   - Impact: Production downtime and quality control challenges
   - Mitigation: Regular maintenance schedules and operator training programs

### Contingency Planning Measures:
- Alternative feedstock sources identification
- Backup equipment suppliers
- Insurance coverage for operational risks
- Emergency response protocols
